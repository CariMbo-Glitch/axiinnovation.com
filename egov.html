eGovS: Electronic Government Savviness

Real-time policy intelligence and stakeholder mapping for strategic advantage in regulated markets

When Policy Shifts, Billions Move

Policy and government affairs decisions reshape entire industries overnight. The IRA legislation alone triggered 38–60% price cuts on the first 10 drugs under negotiation, demonstrating how legislative action directly impacts corporate valuations and market dynamics. Global losses from drug exclusivity changes reach $220 billion, while policy-driven Medicare channel surges can spike prescription volumes by 50% year-over-year.

eGovS aggregates intelligence across government institutions—congressional votes, parliamentary records, health commission activity, FDA staffing changes, and emerging policy narratives—into a single, actionable intelligence platform. Early detection of policy signals prevents reactive scrambling. Stakeholder mapping reveals which actors shape outcomes. Voting-record analysis builds evidence for informed advocacy. Real-time monitoring replaces expensive quarterly consultant reports, delivering institutional intelligence at machine speed.

Core Capabilities

Aggregated Public Intelligence

Consolidates congressional voting records, parliamentary proceedings, health commission activity, FDA staffing changes, and regulatory announcements into a unified dashboard for rapid threat and opportunity assessment.

Ranked Stakeholder Maps

Identifies and ranks the top 10 stakeholders per institution, revealing influence patterns, alliance networks, and decision-making hierarchies to guide strategic engagement.

Real-Time Policy Alerts

Delivers automated notifications when legislative language changes, committee hearings schedule, votes occur, or regulatory timelines shift—enabling proactive strategy adjustment.

Voting-Record Analysis

Tracks historical voting patterns, identifies consistent allies and skeptics, and builds predictive models for upcoming votes on regulatory and pricing legislation.

Policy Impact Modeling

Quantifies the commercial impact of proposed and enacted policies on R&D timelines, market access pathways, reimbursement scenarios, and competitive positioning.

Institutional Benchmarking

Compares your institutional intelligence capabilities against peers, revealing gaps in stakeholder intelligence and emerging policy monitoring needs.
